TEA@UCSD's 2022 Halloween Maze

About

OPERAHOUSE is Themed Entertainment Association at UCSD's 4th annual Halloween horror maze, in collaboration with Tritonfest. This student designed horror maze will be located on campus and free for all UCSD students in Fall 2022. Check out our mazes from previous years:

  • 2021: Wonderland
  • 2019: Petrified
  • 2018: The Last Resort

Get Involved

We are looking for anyone interested in being a part of, or learning about, the design, fabrication, or operation of the maze. There are many open positions for people of all experience levels, if you have any questions feel free to email us at ucsdhauntedmaze@gmail.com

Committee Leads

Committee leads are responsible for leading their committee and working closely with the Executive Team and Creative Director.
